## Best Practices

### Planning Tips

1. **Plan Ahead**: Check weather 3-5 days out
2. **Have Backups**: Alternative targets ready
3. **Be Flexible**: Adapt to conditions
4. **Start Early**: Setup time considered
5. **End Wisely**: Fatigue affects quality
